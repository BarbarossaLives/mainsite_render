Our Mission

At Barbarossa Lives, we forge games, apps, and websites with craftsmanship and independence. We put hard work, creative freedom, and the enduring spirit of independence at the core of everything we create. We’re not here to chase trends. We’re here to build something that lasts.

  • Deliver innovative and custom solutions that solve real business challenges
  • Build games that make use of different AI methods and platforms to offer a more realistic experience.
  • Create games, apps, and web experiences with a focus on lasting quality, creative freedom, and honest work — not chasing trends, but building things meant to endure.
